# Dickinson Tree Company — Brand Design System

## Identity
**Dickinson Tree Company** — Professional arborists, family-run
**Owners:** Reece & Charlotte Dickinson
**Location:** Downley, High Wycombe, Buckinghamshire
**Coverage:** Chilterns — High Wycombe, Marlow, Beaconsfield, Amersham, Maidenhead
**Experience:** 17 years, BS3998 certified, Google Guaranteed 5-star

## Brand Positioning
Family-run professionalism. Not a corporate outfit, not a bloke with a chainsaw. Trusted, insured, qualified — with a genuine passion for trees. The kind of company where Reece answers the phone.

## Colour Philosophy

### Primary: Forest Green (#15803d)
- Trees, nature, growth, healthy canopy
- Trust and environmental responsibility
- The colour of the work itself

### Secondary: Bark Brown (#78350f)
- Earth, timber, natural materials
- Warmth and solidity — grounded
- Heritage and craft

### Accent: Amber (#f59e0b)
- Safety equipment, hi-vis
- Professional signage
- CTAs — "Get a Free Quote"

## Typography
- **Display:** DM Sans — clean, professional, trustworthy
- **Body:** Inter — readable, modern
- **Labels:** Space Grotesk — service specs, pricing

## Themes

### Professional (default)
Clean, trustworthy, modern trade business. For the main website, Google Business presence, quoting.
- Sharp-ish radii — professional, not playful
- Green on white = clean, established
- Strong CTAs in amber — "Book Now", "Free Quote"

### Heritage
Warmer, more traditional. For premium estate/woodland management clients. Emphasises craft and experience.
- Softer radii — approachable
- Deeper greens and browns — established, premium
- Serif display font for authority

## Design Rules
- **Green is nature, not tech** — this isn't a SaaS product. Green = trees, growth, trust
- **Brown grounds it** — prevents the green from feeling too clinical
- **Amber for action** — every CTA should pop in amber/gold
- **Photography first** — beautiful tree work photos sell the service
- **Trust signals prominent** — Google Guaranteed, insurance, BS3998, 17 years

## Services (for content generation)
Tree Felling, Pruning, Hedge Trimming, Stump Removal, Planting, Commercial, Woodland Management, Estate Management, Consultancy, Conservation & TPOs
