# Spool Quote Reframe

**Original:** "Design is the rendering of intent." — Jared Spool (featured in Brad Frost's IDS 2026 talk)

**INC reframe:**
- I = Intent (the human vision)
- N = AI + design system (the bridge)
- C = Design (the rendered output)

**Possible lines:**
- "Design is the rendering of intent. AI is the bridge between them."
- "Intent without a system renders nothing. AI without intent renders noise. The design system connects both."
- "Design is the rendering of intent — but AI is the missing link."
