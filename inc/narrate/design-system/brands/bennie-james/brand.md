# Bennie James — Brand Design System

## Identity
**Bennie James** — Soul DJ, singer-songwriter, multi-instrumentalist
**Market:** New Jersey & Philadelphia — Black music community, weddings, events
**Tagline:** "More hooks than Rex Hunt's tackle box"

## Brand Positioning
Bennie bridges two worlds:
1. **Soul DJ** — NJ/Philly soul, R&B, gospel roots. The Black music scene. Block parties, community events, family celebrations.
2. **Wedding DJ** — Elegant, romantic, celebratory. The soundtrack to the best day of someone's life.

Same artist, different contexts. Same design system, different token sets.

## Colour Philosophy
The palette is built on **warmth** — never cold, never clinical. The soul music tradition is warm amber, deep burgundy, rich gold. No blues, no greys, no tech-brand colours.

### Primary: Amber/Gold (#d97706)
- Warmth, celebration, golden hour, stage lighting
- The colour of soul music album covers from the '60s and '70s
- Connects to Lincoln's warm/L token — family colour

### Secondary: Deep Burgundy (#7c2d12)
- Vinyl records, velvet curtains, red carpet
- The deep richness of soul and R&B
- Romance and depth for wedding context

### Accent: Warm Cream (#f5f0e8)
- Never pure white — always warm
- Wedding stationery, elegant invitations
- Text on dark backgrounds should be cream, not white

## Typography

### Soulful Theme
- **Display:** DM Serif Display — warm serif, sophisticated but not stuffy
- **Body:** Plus Jakarta Sans — friendly, modern
- **Labels:** Space Grotesk — track listings, set times, DJ metadata

### Wedding Theme
- **Display:** Playfair Display — classic elegance, timeless romance
- **Body:** Plus Jakarta Sans
- **Labels:** Inter — clean, professional for booking details

## Themes

### Soulful (default)
The main brand expression. Late-night grooves, vinyl warmth, NJ/Philly soul scene.
- Rounder radii than corporate but not playful
- Gold on dark = spotlight on stage
- Warm-tinted borders, never grey

### Wedding
Elegant, romantic, celebratory. For wedding enquiry pages and couple-facing content.
- Softer radii — romantic, not angular
- Deep gold + rose — rings and romance
- Serif display font for timeless feel
- Ivory backgrounds, never stark white

## Design Rules
- **Never use cold colours** — no blue, no grey, no tech-palette
- **Warm-tint everything** — borders are warm brown, not grey. Shadows are amber-tinted.
- **Cream over white** — text on dark should be #f5f0e8, not #ffffff
- **Gold is the hero** — amber/gold is the primary action colour in both themes
- **Photos should be warm** — apply a warm filter/grade to all photography

## Multi-brand Proof
This brand demonstrates the headless design system approach:
- Same Next.js codebase as lincolnmitchell.io
- Same component library
- Different token set = completely different visual identity
- Proof that one system serves infinite brand expressions
