### INC Framework (Ideate < Narrate > Create)
The core methodology. Maps to the three approach steps on the site:
- **I (Ideate)** — Discovery-first NorthStar prototyping with AI
- **<N> (Narrate)** — The agentic design system infrastructure that amplifies both directions. N = domain knowledge encoded as agentic context. The angle brackets read like an HTML tag — the design system IS the markup that wraps everything.
- **C (Create)** — Production code, content authoring, shipping

N is the expansion layer — not a step between I and C, but the amplifier that radiates into both:
`I <N> C`

### LINC: The Deeper Framework
INC has a deeper layer — LINC — that adds Love/Vision as the foundation. Three layers: process, cognition, virtue.

**Layer 1 — Process (what you do):**
- **L (Love/Vision)** — A calling or conviction that precedes discovery. The NorthStar isn't just a prototype — it's a conviction. This is what the agentic system cannot generate — it comes from the human.
- **I (Ideate)** → **N (Narrate)** → **C (Create)**

**Layer 2 — Cognition (how you think):** Maps to MIND strengths from "The Dyslexic Advantage" (Brock & Fernette Eide):
- **L** = The spark that MIND reasoning can't explain — vision, calling
- **I** = **I**nterconnected + **D**ynamic reasoning — seeing patterns, connections, simulating possibilities
- **N** = **N**arrative reasoning — encoding and communicating vision for different audiences (CEO, CIO, CTO, PO, SME)
- **C** = **M**aterial / **C**reative reasoning — tangible, spatial, buildable. Making it real.

The agentic design system mirrors and enhances these cognitive styles — not replacing them, but amplifying them for people who think in connections, narratives, and possibilities rather than checklists.

**Layer 3 — Virtue (why it matters):** L = the 10 perfections (Dana, Sila, Nekkhamma, Panna, Viriya, Khanti, Sacca, Adhitthana, Metta, Upekkha). As AI takes mechanical work, the human role shifts from doing to guiding — and guidance requires virtue, not just skill. Machine-readable is necessary but not sufficient. The system also needs to be wisdom-guided.

This connects to Anthropic's Constitutional AI approach: humans teach AI to be virtuous → AI reflects virtue back → humans experience patience, honesty, equanimity → the virtuous system creates virtuous users. Same infinity loop.

**Parallel with Helix (MedicalDirector):** Lincoln inverted the biopsychosocial model (Mental, Physical, Social) — surfacing mental health for the patient instead of burying it in the doctor's workflow. Same pattern: the invisible foundation determines whether everything downstream works.

**When to surface:** Lead with INC professionally. Reveal LINC in thought leadership, blog posts, conference talks, and the right conversations. See `memory/project_linc_framework_deep.md` for the full three-layer model.
